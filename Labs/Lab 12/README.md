# Lab 12: Ethics, Fairness, and Bias in Machine Learning

## Objective
The objective of this lab was to understand ethical considerations in machine learning, including fairness, bias, and responsible use of AI systems.

## What is Bias in Machine Learning?
Bias occurs when a machine learning model produces unfair or prejudiced results due to biased training data or flawed assumptions.

## Fairness in AI
Fairness means that a machine learning model should treat all individuals or groups equally without discrimination based on sensitive attributes such as gender, race, or age.

## Ethical Concerns in Machine Learning
Machine learning systems can cause real-world harm if not designed carefully. Common concerns include:
- Biased training data
- Lack of transparency in models
- Misuse of predictive systems
- Privacy issues

## Why Ethics Is Important
Ethical machine learning ensures that AI systems are:
- Fair
- Transparent
- Safe
- Trustworthy

## What I Learned
- How bias can enter machine learning models
- Why fairness is important in AI systems
- The importance of ethical responsibility in data science
- How real-world ML systems must consider social impact

## Conclusion
This lab helped me understand that machine learning is not only about accuracy and performance, but also about fairness, responsibility, and ethical decision-making.
